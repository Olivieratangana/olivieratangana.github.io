This project proposes a secure offline payment architecture for Central Bank Digital Currencies.

The solution combines:

* Smart cards for secure storage
* Smartphones for authentication
* Cryptographic protection mechanisms
* Markov-chain-based fraud prevention
* Double-spending mitigation

The project was presented at ICIN 2025.
