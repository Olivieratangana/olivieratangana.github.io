Block-PAD is a blockchain-enabled framework designed to support secure and resilient CBDC transactions through the integration of digital identity and trusted transaction synchronization mechanisms.

The framework aims to improve:

* Transaction resilience
* Digital identity integration
* Security and privacy
* Flexible transaction management
* Interoperability

The work resulted in a publication in *Computer Networks*.
