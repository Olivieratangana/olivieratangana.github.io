This industry report analyzes the implications of the European Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation (MiCAR) for stablecoins and the broader digital asset ecosystem. It examines regulatory requirements, compliance challenges, market impacts, risk considerations, and strategic perspectives for stablecoin issuers, financial institutions, and digital currency stakeholders operating within the European regulatory landscape.

The report provides industry insights into how MiCAR may influence innovation, governance models, interoperability, digital payment infrastructures, and the future development of regulated digital financial services.
