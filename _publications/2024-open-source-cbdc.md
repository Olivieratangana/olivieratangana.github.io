This industry report explores the role of open-source technologies in the development of Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) systems. It examines the benefits, challenges, governance models, transparency considerations, interoperability aspects, and innovation opportunities associated with open-source approaches in digital currency ecosystems.

The report highlights how open-source development can foster collaboration, trust, auditability, and resilience while supporting the evolution of future CBDC infrastructures.
